Account & Data Deletion - Xpenze

You can delete your account directly in the app:

Settings -> Danger Zone -> Delete Account

This action removes local app data on your device and initiates account removal workflows.

Cannot Log In?

Send a deletion request to:

Email: krixxvalle@gmail.com

Subject: "Xpenze Data Deletion Request"

Include

  • Account email used in Xpenze
  • Optional: user ID
  • "Please delete my account and associated data"

After identity verification, we process requests within a reasonable period (typically within 30 days), except where retention is legally required.
