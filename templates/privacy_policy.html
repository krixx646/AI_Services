Privacy Policy for Xpenze: AI Budget Tracker

Effective date: March 6, 2026

Xpenze ("we", "our", "us") provides an expense and budget tracking app with optional cloud sync, ads for free users, and premium subscription features.

1) Information We Collect

a) Account data: email address, display name, profile photo (if provided).

b) Financial app data you enter: accounts, balances, categories, budgets, transactions, recurring rules, and notes.

c) Optional attachments: receipt reference/metadata when you attach receipts.

d) App and device data: app version, basic diagnostics, analytics events, and identifiers needed for app security and fraud prevention.

e) Subscription/payment data: purchase tokens, subscription status, and entitlement state from Google Play Billing (we do not store your full card details).

2) How We Use Information

  • Provide core app features (tracking, budgets, reports, reminders).
  • Sync your data across devices when cloud sync is enabled.
  • Enable and verify premium subscriptions and trial eligibility.
  • Improve app quality, reliability, and security.
  • Show ads to free-plan users.

3) Services We Use

We may process data through trusted service providers, including:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Functions, Analytics, App Check)
  • Google Play Billing
  • Google AdMob
  • AI provider integrations used by the app for finance coaching features

4) Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data with service providers required to operate the app, legal authorities when required by law, or to protect users and platform security.

5) Data Retention

We keep data while your account is active or as needed to provide the service. You can request deletion at any time (see Section 7).

6) Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards. No system is 100% secure, but we continuously improve protections.

7) Your Rights and Data Deletion

You can delete your account from inside the app:

Settings -> Danger Zone -> Delete Account

If you cannot access the app, request deletion via:

Email: krixxvalle@gmail.com

Subject: "Xpenze Data Deletion Request"

Please include your account email and user ID (if available). We process verified requests within a reasonable period (typically within 30 days).

8) Children

Xpenze is not directed to children under 13 (or equivalent minimum age in your region).

9) Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date.

10) Contact

For privacy questions: Email: krixxvalle@gmail.com
